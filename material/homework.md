# Homework

The goal of this homework is to **prepare the final group presentation** and **finalize your mini-project**.

Please **create a separate directory** in your group’s **Google Drive folder** (under **Week 8**) and upload the following deliverables:

## Deliverables

### 1. Finalized Jupyter Notebook

* Make sure the project is complete and ready for presentation.

### 2. Presentation Slides

* Prepare a presentation of approximately **8–10 minutes**.
* Clearly describe the project.
* Here is a [minimum template for the presentation](./template.md) to help you structure your slides and discussion.
* Be sure to include:
  * The contributions of each team member.
  * The challenges faced during the project.
  * What you learned during the mini-project.

* Live Demo of Your Mini-Project During the Presentation
  * Start by showing the prototype designed in Phase 1.
  * Show the working demo developed for Phase 2.
  * Show the working demo developed for Phase 3.
  * Compare the outcomes of Phase 2 and Phase 3.

### 4. Short Report – "The 4Ls" Reflection

Write a short reflective report addressing the following four points:

* **Loved:** What went well during the project?
  (What you appreciated or enjoyed.)
* **Loathed:** What did not go well?
  (Gaps, difficulties, or aspects that could be improved.)
* **Longed for:** What did you wish you had during the project?
  (Resources, tools, time, etc.)
* **Learned:** What new knowledge or skills did you gain?
  (Technical, teamwork, communication, etc.)

> [!NOTE]
>
> * Focus on **clarity** and **highlighting the main points** during the presentation.
> * The short report should be concise but reflective.
